Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Need help with our delivery services? Here are some answers to common questions.

What is the delivery time for my package?

Delivery times depend on the location of the sender and the recipient. We offer same-day and next-day delivery options for local shipments and standard delivery for longer distances.

How can I track my delivery?

You can track your package in real-time by entering your tracking number on our "Track My Package" page. You'll receive email or SMS updates as your package moves through our system.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We offer international shipping to a wide range of countries. Shipping costs and times vary depending on the destination, package size, and service selected.

How do I request a delivery quote?

To get a delivery quote, simply visit our "Get a Quote" page and provide the details of your package, including size, weight, and destination. We will send you a customized price quote based on your needs.

What happens if my package is delayed?

If your package is delayed, we will notify you via email or SMS. In most cases, delays are due to external factors like weather or customs. We’ll keep you updated and do our best to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
